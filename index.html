About Me

Born on October 20, 1998, I am a Tanzanian artist, songwriter, website & app developer, and entrepreneur professionally known as Reykee.

My sound, Afrobongo, fuses Swahili heritage with Afrobeat, Dancehall, Pop, and Reggae. Beyond music, I build scalable digital platforms and brands that merge creativity, technology, and real-world impact.
